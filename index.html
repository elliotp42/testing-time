Mountain Biking is a hobby that I picked since learning how to bike at the age of 13. I genuinely believe that Mountain Biking is one of the best hobbies that anyone can have, as people of any skill can go mountain biking, and it's a fun way to get excercise in and explore nature.

Intro to Mountain Biking

Types of trails and difficulty levels

There are various skill levels and styles of trails that you can consider when you go biking.

As far as skill ratings go, there are typically Green (Easy), Blue (Intermediate), Black Diamond (Difficult), and Double Black Diamond (Expert)

There are also multiple styles of trails, from fast-paced jump trails, to tech trails that challenge your skills of maneuvering on your bike, and then my personal favorite type: flow trails. Flow trails don't require you to have a ton of skill, but instead let's you just go as fast as possible.

Gear needed to get started

The best part about mountain biking, is that while you can spend thousands of dollars to get a really nice bike, a solid helmet, a water bottle, and a random bike from your garage is all you really need to get started.
